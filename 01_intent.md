# Intent

Intent is the foundational layer of CAAF IF.

Every content asset exists to serve a **clear and explainable purpose**.
If intent is unclear, structural and content decisions become arbitrary.

## Definition of intent

Intent answers the question:

> What must this content enable the reader to understand, decide, or do?

Intent is **not**:
- a feature list
- a marketing goal
- a vague topic description

Intent must be:
- explicit
- testable
- stable over time

## Intent as a constraint

Intent acts as a structural constraint.
It limits what belongs into a content asset and what does not.

Clear intent reduces:
- unnecessary complexity
- redundant information
- structural drift
