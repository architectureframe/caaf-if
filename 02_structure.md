# Structure

Structure is the visible expression of intent.

In CAAF IF, structure describes **how information is ordered, grouped, and
sequenced** to make intent understandable.

## Structural principles

A valid structure:
- reflects intent directly
- follows a logical progression
- avoids hidden or implicit assumptions

Structure is evaluated independently of:
- visual design
- CSS frameworks
- layout technologies

## Structural clarity

Structural clarity exists when a reader can:
- anticipate what comes next
- explain why a section exists
- understand relationships between sections
